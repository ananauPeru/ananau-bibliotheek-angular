Create a new exercise

Created: 21/05/2024 | Last Update: 21/05/2024

If you want to create a new exercise, you can click on "Create exercise" in the menu or on the dashboard.


You will get a form. Fill in all the required fields and upload a document by clicking in the upload zone. Select the document you wish to upload. If you want to upload multiple documents, that is also possible. After uploading, you will see the documents loading in the upload zone.


After creating an exercise, you will be redirected to a page with the details of the created exercise. Here, you can assign the exercise to a Spanish learner.


In the appearing window, you need to choose the deadline, which should be in the future. Then, you can choose a student from the list of Spanish learners.


There you go! Now the student will see the exercise assigned to them in the list and complete it.
